Introduction

Java Club

Java Club is a coffee shop in San Francisco that was founded by two web developers that quit their jobs to start a coffee shop. The shop is located in North Beach and is a quite place to study in a busy city!

Target audience

The target audience is San Francisco coffee lovers who need a cozy place to study, read, or code!

Their primary goals are to drink yummy coffee and tea and be able to work and study in a nice environment.

About Us

The goal of Java club has always been to cultivate an enviroment that encourages creativity. In the past 20 years that we've been serving San Francisco locals, we believe we've done just that. When we quit our jobs as web developers in 2002 to pursue our dream of opening a coffee shop, we dreamed of empowering the community we love so much. We hope our space is place for you to relax, sit back, and enjoy some java (coffee & coding).
